**Badge** — small pill for status and metadata; six semantic tints (`ghost`, `purple`, `green`, `red`, `amber`, `cyan`).

```jsx
<Badge variant="green" dot>Live</Badge>
<Badge variant="purple">Twitch</Badge>
<Badge variant="amber">Hiatus</Badge>
```

Pass `dot` for a leading status dot. Keep labels short; content is often uppercase.
